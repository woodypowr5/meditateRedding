A retreat removes the daily buzz of modern life and gives one an opportunity to dedicate one-hundred percent of their effort and attention to spiritual exploration. Most of them involve numberous meditation sessions throughout the day and are a great way to rejuvenate your practice. If you've never been to a retreat and are hesitant, try out one of the shorter retreats before you make the dive into a longer retreat.
